# Obrike Oghenekome Timothy

Welcome to my digital garden! 🌱

I am a systems engineer and polymath who operates best at the extremes. For me, engineering isn't just about staying comfortably in the application layer. It is about understanding the entire spectrum. I bounce between writing compilers, simulating CPU schedulers, architecting cloud sandboxes, and diving into theoretical physics. 

Recently, an academic excursion to the **Dangote Refineries** turned into an upcoming internship offer—a fun story of geeking out over physical infrastructure that actually paid off! I am currently sorting out the logistics, but this garden is where I will be documenting that journey, alongside my technical projects, research papers, and random late-night thoughts.

Whether you are a recruiter, a fellow engineer, or just someone who stumbled down a rabbit hole, I am glad you are here.

### 💻 The Cloud & Infrastructure
I build tools to solve my own bottlenecks, refactoring messy logic into modular, clean architecture.
* 🌩️ **[Cloud-IDE](https://github.com/t569/cloud-ide):** A distributed development environment featuring a custom sandbox control plane and secure container isolation.
* 🧠 **[4lorn](https://github.com/t569/4lorn):** A repository progress tracker and knowledge graph engine leveraging Python and ChromaDB to prevent metadata drift.
* 🤝 **Collaborations:** Built alongside brilliant peers, including AI-driven logic in **[blockscribe-ai](https://github.com/yensama7/blockscribe-ai)** and the **[noodle](https://github.com/NnannaOmoke/noodle)** project.
* 🌐 **Enterprise Networks:** Designed a multi-layered, redundant fiber backbone for GTBank Plc using Cisco Packet Tracer. 

### ⚙️ The Core Mechanics (Systems & Parsers)
I don't just use languages; I study how they execute.
* **[MLFQScheduler](https://github.com/t569/MLFQScheduler):** Implementation of a Multi-Level Feedback Queue, simulating how operating systems handle process scheduling.
* **Compilers & Interpreters:** Actively building and experimenting with language design through **[MiniBuild](https://github.com/t569/MiniBuild)**, **[Kyle](https://github.com/t569/Kyle)**, and **[tracer](https://github.com/t569/tracer)**.
* **[BMP](https://github.com/t569/BMP):** Low-level bitmap parsing and image manipulation.

### 🛠️ The Bare Metal & The Classics
Because software is only as fun as the silicon it runs on.
* **Embedded Robotics:** Engineered an Arduino Uno obstacle-detection robot using ultrasonic sensors.
* **Low-Level Systems:** Actively researching a custom Real-Time Operating System (RTOS) from scratch for ATmega microcontrollers.
* **Studying the Greats:** I frequently fork and dissect industry-defining code, from the data indexing architecture of **[cocoindex](https://github.com/t569/cocoindex)** to the ultimate bare-metal engineering of the **[Apollo-11 AGC source code](https://github.com/t569/Apollo-11)**.
